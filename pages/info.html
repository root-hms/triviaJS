Info

  1. You can only enter your name once per game, if you want to change it you will have to restart the game.
  2. In quick start mode, depending on the level of difficulty chosen, you will score points as follows
    • Easy: 1 point
    • Medium: 3 point
    • Hard: 5 point
  3. In the custom mode, depending on the level of difficulty chosen, you will obtain points as mentioned above, if you do not choose a difficulty, it will be random and will add the corresponding points
  4. You will only appear in the ranking if the score obtained is higher than those already on the list. If your name already appeared on it, it wont be overwritten with the new score.
